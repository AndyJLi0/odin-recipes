Chinese Dumplings

"Know thy self, know thy enemy. A thousand battles, a thousand victories."

Here we have the famous chinse dumplings. This recipe has been passed down by generations and generations.

Chinese Dumplings are a popular dish in China. They are among (us) a variety of popular dishes commonly eaten aroudn Chinese New Year. Impress your friends with this godlike recipe.

Ingredients
  • For dough, you need:
    • 225g plain flour
    • 150ml cold water
  • For filling:
    • 1/2 bunch of coriander
    • 2 spring onions
    • 1 cube of ginger
    • 1 clove of garlic
    • 5 chinese mushrooms
    • 1 chinese chive
    • 200g pork mince
  • For Marinade:
    • 2 teaspoons of seasame oil
    • 2 tablespoons of soysauce
    • 1/4 teaspoons of sugar

Instructions:

  1. chop all ingredients into similar size pieces, then place in a bowl and mix well with the marinade.
  2. For the wrap, place filling into the centre of the dough
  3. Fold the bottom of the dough up, and then pinch the 2 corners to create the dumpling shape
  4. Deep fry at 180 degrees celcius for around 4-5 minutes
  5. place in vegetable oil and throw in the dumplings
  6. reduce the heat and periodically pour water into the pan, covering the lid right after you do so
  7. once the pan is dry, your dumplings are ready to be served!!!
